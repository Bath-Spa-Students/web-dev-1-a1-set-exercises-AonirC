SIMPLE CRINKLE COOKIES RECIPE

Here is short video of the delicious pastry recipe from the Betty Crocker channel,video Go and check it out!

Step by step Recipe:

  • Add oil, vanilla extract, eggs, cocoa powder and sugar. Whisk it!
  • Add flour, baking powder and salt in a seperate bowl. Whisk it!
  • Add both of the bowls together. Whisk it!
  • Cover the dough with parchment paper/clingwrap, then refrigerate it for 3 hours or overnight.
